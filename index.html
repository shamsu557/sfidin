Our Founder

Sarauniya Fanna, a visionary leader with a passion for community development and social welfare, established the Sarauniya Fanna Initiative for Development in Nigeria (SFIDIN) with a mission to transform lives and communities across Nigeria.

With over 15 years of experience in community service and development work, Sarauniya Fanna has dedicated her life to uplifting the underprivileged, supporting orphans, and creating sustainable development initiatives that empower individuals and communities.

Her journey began in her hometown, where she witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by vulnerable populations. This experience ignited her passion for social change and led to the establishment of SFIDIN, which has since grown into a respected organization making significant impact across Nigeria.

Under her leadership, SFIDIN has implemented numerous successful programs in education, healthcare, skills acquisition, and economic empowerment, touching thousands of lives and creating lasting positive change.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged individuals and communities through sustainable development initiatives, quality education, healthcare support, and economic empowerment programs that foster self-reliance and dignity.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to opportunities for growth, development, and a dignified life, contributing to a more equitable and prosperous society.
