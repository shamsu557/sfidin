About the Initiative

The Sarauniya Fanna Initiative for Development in Nigeria (SFIDIN) is a non-profit, non-political, and community-based organization committed to transforming lives and building sustainable communities across Nigeria.

Established in 2015 by a visionary leader, Hajiya TMP Chief Fanna Muhammad Kawu, SFIDIN champions initiatives that address the root causes of poverty, inequality, and underdevelopment. Our programs are tailored to empower the most vulnerable groups—especially women, youth, and orphans.

Our core areas of focus include:

  • Education: Scholarships, school sponsorships, and Islamic education for orphans and the less privileged.
  • Healthcare: Traditional and modern health support, community wellness programs, and access to affordable treatment.
  • Skills Acquisition: Vocational training in tailoring, herbal medicine, ICT, crafts, and more.
  • Economic Empowerment: Micro-grants, mentorship, and startup support for young entrepreneurs and women.

SFIDIN believes in inclusive development and partners with local communities, traditional leaders, government agencies, and international stakeholders to drive change from the grassroots. Every project we undertake is guided by empathy, accountability, and sustainability.

Through compassion, vision, and action, we are building a Nigeria where no one is left behind.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged individuals and communities through sustainable development initiatives, quality education, healthcare support, and economic empowerment programs that foster self-reliance and dignity.

We are committed to creating opportunities for growth and development, particularly for orphans and vulnerable children, ensuring they have access to the resources and support they need to thrive.

Through our work, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create lasting positive change in the lives of those we serve.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to opportunities for growth, development, and a dignified life, contributing to a more equitable and prosperous society.

We envision communities where basic needs are met, human rights are respected, and every person has the chance to reach their full potential.

Our vision is of a society where compassion, solidarity, and social justice prevail, and where sustainable development benefits all members of the community.

What We Do

Our programs and initiatives focus on creating sustainable solutions to address the needs of underprivileged communities.

Education Support

We provide scholarships, educational materials, and infrastructure support to ensure quality education for orphans and underprivileged children.

Healthcare Services

Our healthcare initiatives include medical outreach programs, health education, and support for healthcare facilities in underserved communities.

Orphan Support

We provide comprehensive support for orphans, including shelter, nutrition, education, healthcare, and emotional support.

Skills Acquisition

Our vocational training programs equip young adults with practical skills that enhance their employability and entrepreneurial capabilities.

Community Development

We implement various community development projects that improve infrastructure, sanitation, and access to clean water.

Economic Empowerment

Our microfinance and business development programs help individuals and families achieve financial independence and stability.

Who We Are

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach our work with empathy and understanding, recognizing the dignity of every individual we serve.
  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations, ensuring transparency and accountability.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all our programs and services, continuously improving to better serve our communities.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of partnerships and work closely with various stakeholders to maximize our impact.
  • Sustainability: We develop solutions that create lasting change and empower communities to become self-reliant.

Our Approach

At SFIDIN, we believe in a holistic, community-centered approach to development. We recognize that sustainable change requires addressing multiple interconnected factors that contribute to poverty and inequality.

Our programs are designed with input from the communities we serve, ensuring they address real needs and are culturally appropriate. We emphasize capacity building and knowledge transfer, equipping individuals and communities with the skills and resources they need to drive their own development.

We regularly monitor and evaluate our programs to measure their impact and make necessary adjustments. This commitment to learning and adaptation allows us to continuously improve our effectiveness and reach more people in need.
